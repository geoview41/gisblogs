## Visualizing Strava Activities

These Strava activity snapshots show different types of routes recorded across Switzerland. The heatmap reveals fascinating patterns based on how strava user move , for instance walking, biking, swimming, or skiing.

### Walk – Lausanne  
![Strava walk in Lausanne](/strava/strava_walk_lausanne.png)  
In Lausanne, it's interesting to see that walking and biking routes aren't the same. Some paths are only used on foot — like ones with stairs or areas that are too crowded for bikes.

### Bike – Lausanne  
![Strava bike in Lausanne](/strava/strava_bike_lausanne.png)

### Swim – Thun  
![Strava swim in Thun](/strava/strava_natation_thun.png)  
In Thun, the swim trace clearly shows the Ironman parcours. It really stands out.

### Ski touring – Valais  
![Strava ski route in Valais](/strava/strava_skivalais.png)  
In the ski touring map, you can see which mountain routes are more used. Some areas are clearly more popular or easier to access than others.
